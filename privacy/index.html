Privacy Policy

How ClearPour handles your data.

Last updated: April 23, 2026

What ClearPour stores

ClearPour stores the drinks and settings you enter, including beverage names, categories, size, alcohol percentage, timestamps, and your daily target if you choose to set one.

How your data is stored

ClearPour stores your data locally on your device.

If iCloud is available and enabled for ClearPour, your data may also sync through your private iCloud account using Apple’s CloudKit services so it can appear on your other Apple devices.

Payments

ClearPour offers a one-time in-app purchase to unlock unlimited saved drinks.

Purchases are processed by Apple through the App Store. ClearPour does not collect or store your payment card details.

ClearPour uses Apple’s StoreKit framework to determine whether the unlimited saved drinks purchase has been made or restored.

Analytics and advertising

ClearPour does not use third-party advertising.

ClearPour does not use third-party analytics services to track your activity across apps or websites.

Your choices

You can edit or delete drinks and beverages from within the app.

If you no longer want ClearPour data to sync with iCloud, you can disable iCloud access for the app in your Apple device settings.

Contact

If you have privacy questions about ClearPour, contact support@clearpour.app.

ClearPour is a personal drink tracker for awareness, not medical advice.
