Support

Questions, bugs, or feature requests.

Email support@clearpour.app and include your device model, iOS version, and a short description of the issue if you can.

Frequently asked questions

What does the full version unlock?

The full version unlocks unlimited saved drinks, weekly goals, custom day and week boundaries, and alternate app icons.

How do I restore my purchase?

Open the upgrade screen in ClearPour and tap Restore Purchase. Make sure you are signed into the same Apple Account used for the original purchase.

Does ClearPour sync across devices?

Yes. ClearPour stores data locally and can sync through your private iCloud account when iCloud is available.

Do I have to set a daily alcohol target?

No. The daily target is optional. ClearPour works well without one if you just want to log drinks and review your history without a max.

Does ClearPour estimate BAC?

No. ClearPour tracks what you drank, not your blood alcohol concentration. It does not determine whether it is safe to drive.

Can I delete my data?

Yes. You can delete drinks and beverages from inside the app. If you want to stop syncing, you can also remove ClearPour’s iCloud data from your Apple account settings.
