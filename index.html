Why it feels different

Counts alcohol, not just drinks.

A 12 oz beer and a heavy liquor pour are not the same thing. ClearPour tracks size and alcohol percentage so your log reflects what you actually had.

Built for tracking, not scolding.

No sobriety program. No guilt. No pretend life coaching. Just a better way to keep tabs on your own drinking.

Features

What ClearPour does

  • Save your usual beers, wines, cocktails, and pours
  • Log drinks fast from your personal drink list
  • See your daily total at a glance
  • Review your history by day
  • Set a personal daily target if you want one
  • Edit mistakes and keep your log accurate
  • Sync privately across your Apple devices with iCloud
  • Unlock unlimited saved drinks with a one-time purchase

Pricing

Free to start. One-time unlock.

ClearPour is free with up to 5 saved drinks. You can keep logging those saved drinks forever.

A one-time in-app purchase unlocks unlimited saved drinks. No subscription.

Important note

ClearPour is for personal tracking only. It does not estimate BAC and it does not tell you whether it is safe to drive.

ClearPour is a personal drink tracker for awareness, not medical advice.
