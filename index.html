Why people will care

Counts alcohol, not just drinks.

A lager, a martini, and a neat pour should not all count the same. ClearPour tracks size and alcohol percentage so the totals match the actual drink.

Made for awareness, not shame.

No lectures, no performative wellness, no quitting program. Just a cleaner way to keep tabs on your own drinking.
